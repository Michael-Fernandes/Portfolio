How did I end up here?

I am an UX Designer with experience in both Design Research and UX/UI design. In the past I have done work in domains such as Healthcare, Civil Tech, Enterprise and E-Commerce. I am interested in Personal Informatics, Data Visualization and Interaction Design. My experiences in the UX field thus far has led me to believe that a good user experience is one that engages an user in a way that leads to better decision making, more delight and leaves the user feeling more human not less.

I am currently seeking opportunities in UX Design and Research (or a combined position) and have a passion for building my coding skills. If you have any questions please reach out directly.
